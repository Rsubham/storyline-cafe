About US

DISCOVER STORYLINE CAFE

Crafting Experiences by the Sea

By the Bay of Bengal in Gopalpur, Storyline Café is more than a café. It is a coastal retreat where dining, stays, and celebrations come alive in one place, inviting you to slow down and enjoy life by the sea.

Our rooftop restaurant brings you flavors from around the world, paired with breathtaking ocean views. From your first morning coffee to a starlit dinner, every meal is an experience designed to be savored.

For those who stay with us, Storyline offers cozy rooms and cottages filled with comfort and warmth. Spend the day by the pool, share stories around a bonfire, or simply enjoy the peace of a seaside morning. With Wi-Fi, concierge, rentals, and round-the-clock care, your stay feels effortless.

Storyline is also where memories are made. Birthdays, weddings, pre-weddings, date nights, and pool parties find their perfect setting here. With thoughtful touches and personalized arrangements, every celebration becomes unforgettable.

WHY STORYLINE

The Bay’s Premier Destination
